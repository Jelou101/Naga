About Naga City

Naga City, Cebu, is a progressive city with a rich history and a dynamic culture. Officially chartered in 2007, the city has grown into a hub of industry, tourism, and community life.

Its annual **Dagitab Festival**, celebrated on December 23, highlights its unique identity as the “City of Lights.” Visitors can explore historical landmarks, natural attractions like Mount Naupa, and diverse cultural offerings.

Naga also plays a vital role in the region’s industrial development, housing major energy facilities while maintaining its environmental commitment.
